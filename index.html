About Me

Hi, I’m Ella Serafinowicz. I'm 21, I was born on the 21st of August and a student studying IMD at Algonquin College. and last summer I discovered that I love going on solo trips. I recently went to Toronto, where I spent most of my time exploring downtown and even ran into a few local micro-celebrities along with visiting a friend nearby in Milton. I'm keeping the momentum going this year with plans to visit Alcatraz Island in San Francisco and also made plans to see a family friend in Slocan, British Columbia. It’s going to be a great summer.


When summer hits Gatineau, you can usually find me cruising around the city with my dog in shotgun, just soaking in the weather. I'm also a huge fan of fishing, to the point where my rods lives in my car incase a body of water is gonna be nearby. My dog Bells is the best fishing partner and my good luck charm.
